Mastermind

Guess the sequence

The computer geneterates a sequence of 4 colors taken from a palette of 3, 4, 5 or 6 colors, depending on the difficulty level. The player has to guess the right sequence. Every time the player chooses 4 colors a visual hint has given. White dot means the player guessed the right color in the right position, while black dot means the playes guessed the right color but in the wrong position.
